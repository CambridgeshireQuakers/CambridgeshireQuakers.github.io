Cambridgeshire Area Meeting of the Religious Society of Friends (Quakers)

Welcome

This is the website for the Cambridgeshire Area Meeting of the Religious Society of Friends (Quakers) in Britain.

The Area Meeting brings together eight Local Meetings in Cambridgeshire (listed below), and gathers several times a year. Area Meetings are the "primary meeting for church affairs", and we meet to discuss matters of common concern, to share our successes and challenges, to build a welcoming community beyond the Local Meeting and to connect us with the wider family of Friends.

Local Quaker Meetings in Cambridgeshire

Each of the meetings in Cambridgeshire meet on Sunday mornings. Some also have regular Meetings for Worship at other times during the week. Click on the name of the meeting for more details, including directions, accessibility information and arrangements for children.
